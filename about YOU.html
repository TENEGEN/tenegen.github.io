You are the brightest light.
Not just lighting up this room, or this space.
You are the brightest light in the world...
In this entire creation.
The world is not shining in upon you.
You shine out, illuminating it.
The creation is not giving you form and place.
You give the creation form and place.
This website does not impress you
Or make you think you need something from it.
This is an example of how much TENGEN needs you.
TENGEN only exists in the light
—you—
shine to make TENGEN real.
If you do not look
There is nothing to see.
If you do not listen—
TENGEN can not be.
Thank you, light giver,
Life giver.
Thank you for being here.
TENGEN is alive in the sound
Because you hear.
